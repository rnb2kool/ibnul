Objective

Md. Ibnul Bin Kader Arnub is an experienced cybersecurity analyst with over 7 years of expertise in Information Technology. Holding an MSc in Electrical and Electronic Engineering and an MBA in Finance, he specializes in enhancing system resilience and developing innovative strategies for protecting critical infrastructure. Arnub's current research focuses on cybersecurity challenges in autonomous vehicles, phishing detection using AI and machine learning & Securing Core Banking System (CBS). As an active researcher, Arnub has published multiple papers and continues to explore more. His commitment to advancing cybersecurity knowledge and practices reflects his dedication to making the world more secure. Arnub is also passionate about contributing to academic and industry research, aiming to bridge the gap between practical solutions and theoretical advancements in cybersecurity.
